Why Vietnam?

One of Southeast Asia's fastest-growing health & wellness markets

📈 $1.2B Market by 2027

Vietnam's nutraceutical and health supplement market is projected to reach USD 1.2 billion by 2027, growing at ~12% CAGR driven by rising health awareness and a growing middle class.

👥 98 Million Consumers

A young, urbanizing population with 70% under 45 years old. Consumer spending on health & beauty is growing 3x faster than general FMCG categories.

🛒 E-commerce Explosion

Vietnam's e-commerce grew 25% in 2024. Shopee, Lazada, and TikTok Shop are primary channels for health products, with brand authenticity being a key purchase driver.

🏥 Premium Brand Preference

Vietnamese consumers strongly prefer imported, certified brands. Foreign-origin products command 40-60% price premium over domestic equivalents, especially in pharmaceuticals and supplements.

Partnership Process

A streamlined 4-step process from first contact to market launch — typically 4–8 weeks

1

Initial Contact & NDA

Submit the partnership inquiry form below. We'll respond within 48 hours with an NDA and initial questions about your brand, product range, and distribution goals.

2

Due Diligence & Market Assessment

We conduct a market assessment for your brand in Vietnam, including competitive analysis, regulatory requirements, and pricing strategy. We present a preliminary distribution plan with projected volume forecasts.

3

Distribution Agreement

Negotiate and sign a distribution agreement covering exclusivity territory, minimum order commitments, pricing structure, marketing obligations, and brand guidelines compliance.
